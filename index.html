Products

  • Ice cream

    Ice cream is a mixture of milk, cream, sugar, and sometimes other ingredients that has been frozen into a soft, creamy delight using special

    techniques. Ice cream has been a popular treat for hundreds of years but has only become commonplace since the widespread use of refrigeration.

  • ice coffee

    Iced coffee is a popular choice in summer months providing a chilled alternative to hot drinks. While drinking coffee is associated with an

    increase in alertness, there is no research to suggest any differences between the beverage when served hot or chilled. Iced coffee is typically prepared using coffee, ice, dairy or plant-based milk, and sometimes sugar.

  • milkshakes

    A milkshake is a sweet, cold beverage that is usually made from milk, ice cream, or iced milk, and flavorings or sweeteners such as

    butterscotch, caramelsauce, chocolate syrup, or fruit syrup. It can also be made by adding powder into fresh milk and stirring the powder into the milk. Today, milkshakes are widely sold in fast food restaurants and diners alike
